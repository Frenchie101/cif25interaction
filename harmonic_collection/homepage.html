Welcome to My Site

Come explore color with me, through the lense of colorblindness. This collection of entries dives into the experience of color from my perspective as a colorblind individual. Each entry explores different facets of color perception, representation, and the emotional and psychological impacts of living in a world designed for those with typical color vision. Whether through art, personal anecdotes, or scientific insights, I invite you to join me on this colorful journey.
